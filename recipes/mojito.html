MOJITO

A Refreshing Cuban Classic

The Mojito is a timeless classic with deep roots in Cuban history. While its exact origins are difficult to pinpoint, it is widely believed to have evolved from a medicinal drink used by sailors to stave off illness, combining lime, mint, and sugar to combat ailments. By the 19th century, it had transformed into the refreshing cocktail we know today. The drink gained massive international popularity in the mid-20th century, particularly following the rise of Cuban tourism and the global fascination with Caribbean culture. It is now considered the definitive refreshing summer cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp granulated sugar
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Muddle the mint leaves and sugar in a glass.
  2. Add the rum and lime juice.
  3. Fill the glass with ice.
  4. Stir well to combine.

Enjoy responsibly!
